How to Play

use "WASD" to move,
SPACE to teleport towards the mouse.
Use Enter to chat with other playes
Killing another player will give you 1 health
Left click to shoot a bullet
and Right click to bring up your shield.
Teleporting - 3 energy
shooting/shield - 1 energy

Player Commands

To enter a command first open chat with the enter key
next you add a "@" symbol to the begining of the message
this indecates to the server that this text is a command
then you can use one of the commands that are in the game
there are currently 4 commands:
@addbot (adds 1 bot ; max 9 bots)
@removebot (removes 1 random bot)
@mapsize+ (increases the map size)
@mapsize- (decreases the map size)
